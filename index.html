How to Play:

Goal: Reveal the glass sculpture by removing all ice voxels

Ice: Breaks on 2nd tap (blue, "thud" sound)

Unstable Ice: Breaks on 2nd tap, explodes nearby cubes! (pulsing orange)

Glass: Breaks on 3rd tap (clear, "tink" sound)

Chain Breaking: Breaking ice weakens adjacent ice cubes

Camera: Drag to rotate and view different angles

Perfect Score: Don't break any glass voxels!
